Frequently asked questions

Can ElevenLabs agents handle licensed insurance interactions?

+

Agents handle the pre-licensed workflow including structured intake, triage, and factual servicing. Anything requiring licensed advice or policy authority routes to your licensed team with full context and a complete transcript.

Can ElevenLabs agents integrate with our claims management system?

+

Yes. Pre-built connectors for Duck Creek, Guidewire, Majesco, Applied Epic, and Vertafore plus REST API and webhook integration for custom systems.

How can agents support fraud reduction?

+

Consistent structured intake is the foundation of effective fraud detection. Agents capture the same required fields on every FNOL call and every transcript is timestamped and searchable, enabling SIU teams to cross-reference narratives against policy history.

What happens when an agent reaches the limit of what it can handle?

+

Escalation logic is fully configurable. When a call exceeds scope it transfers to a human agent with the full transcript and call summary already complete. No cold transfers and no repeated information.
